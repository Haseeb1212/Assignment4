As far as the line follower concept goes. It is not really that big of a task. It does not require hardcore coding nor does it require complex algorithms. Simple co-ordinate geometry, basic microcontroller concepts like PWM, DC Motor interface and concept of GPIO is far more than enough. As far as the sensors go, usually line follower requires contrast detectors which you can buy from a nearby hobby shop or build one on a small piece of pcb as it requires a LED, a photo detector diode and a resistance to limit voltage and current.
